Act 1

Scene 1

in the beginning of this scene, Paris is talking to Friar Lawrence about the wedding when Juliet enters, refusing to marry paris. Juliet argues with paris and leaves to talk with Friar Lawrence. they talk for a little bit and Friar Lawrence comes up with a plan. he gives juliet a sleeping potion and tells her to take it on Wednesday night so that her family would think she was dead. then she would be brought to the Capulets' tomb where Romeo could pick her up. Friar Lawrence sends Friar John to tell Romeo of the plan.

Scene 2

Capulet is preparing a wedding party and juliet enters, telling him she has repented her sin of opposition and agrees to marry Paris.

Scene 3

Juliet and Nurse are deciding on wedding clothes, Lady Capulet enters to wish her daughter good night and Nurse and Lady Capulet leave. Juliet puts down a dagger in case the sleeping potion doesn't work. she takes the sleeping potion and falls asleep.

Scene 4

The Capulets are still preparing for the party. Capulet tells Nurse to wake up Juliet.

Scene 5

Nurse enters Juliet's chambers to find her asleep. she tries to wake her up but does not succeed and sees that Juliet is "dead". Capulet and Lady Capulet enter to find their daughter "dead". Paris and Friar Lawrence enter with musicians to fetch Juliet to find the same thing. everyone leaves to prepare for what is now a funeral.
